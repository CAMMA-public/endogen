Endoscopic Surgery Generative AI

Research Group CAMMA, University of Strasbourg

Endogen is to revolutionize surgical data creation by bringing an unprecedented level of realism to medical professionals, researchers, and learners worldwide. We are dedicated to creating a cutting-edge AI generative model that crafts surgical data with unparalleled precision.
